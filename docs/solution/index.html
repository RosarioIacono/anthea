Our Solution

Our Solution: A Hybrid Platform for Open-Source Seedlings#

Anthea combines three elements into a single platform:

1. Vertical Nursery as Precision Lab#

Our vertical nursery allows us to:

  • control light spectrum, intensity, and photoperiod,
  • modulate temperature, humidity, VPD, and CO₂,
  • standardise nutrient supply and irrigation.

This makes early growth repeatable and optimisable.

2. Biological Enhancement#

We inoculate seedlings with beneficial microbes and mycorrhizal fungi, aiming to:

  • improve nutrient acquisition,
  • enhance stress tolerance,
  • support healthier soil–root interactions after transplanting.

3. Regenerative Orchard as Field Lab#

The nursery is embedded in a regenerative apple orchard that functions as a living testing ground:

  • we expose seedlings and varieties to real climatic and soil variation,
  • we monitor performance over seasons,
  • we design for agroecological resilience, not just short-term yield.

Anthea’s solution is not just a different space to grow plants — it is a new interface between precision horticulture and regenerative farming, underpinned by open-source genetics.
